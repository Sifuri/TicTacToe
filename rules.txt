
You are currently playing 'Tic Tac Toe' 

The game requires two players with the following options: Human Vs Machine or Human Vs Human. 

The game is played with two board pieces, 'X' and 'O', the player to go first will be 'X'. 

The objective is for each player to align 3 of their pieces diagonally, in a row, or column. The first player to do so, wins. If the board is filled with all pieces and no player has won, the game will be declared a tie. 

Above is a display of the Tic Tac Toe board with each empty space numbered 0-8, selecting a number from this range during gameplay will replace the empty space with the players game piece.
